Why Dine with Us?

At Mughlai Adda, we celebrate the vibrant, plant-based traditions of India, offering a delightful array of dishes that are not only wholesome but full of flavor. Located in a cozy corner of the city, we are committed to serving you the finest vegetarian fare crafted from fresh, locally sourced ingredients, and traditional recipes from across India.

  • 100% Vegetarian: Our entire menu is strictly vegetarian, crafted to satisfy even the most discerning food lovers.
  • Fresh & Healthy: Every dish is made fresh to order using natural spices and ingredients.
  • Vegan & Gluten-Free Options: A variety of vegan and gluten-free dishes that do not compromise on taste.
  • Traditional Recipes: Honoring time-tested vegetarian recipes from various regions of India.
  • Eco-Friendly Dining: We offer sustainable dining with biodegradable packaging and organic ingredients.

Our Specialty Dishes

Palak Paneer

Fresh cottage cheese cubes simmered in a vibrant spinach gravy, seasoned with spices to perfection.

Veg Biryani

Aromatic basmati rice cooked with seasonal vegetables, saffron, and our special house-made biryani masala.

Paneer Butter Masala

A rich, creamy dish made with paneer in a velvety tomato-based sauce, bursting with flavor.

Masala Dosa

A crispy rice and lentil crepe filled with a spiced potato mixture, served with tangy coconut chutney and warm sambar.

Rajma Chawal

A comforting bowl of kidney beans cooked in a spicy tomato gravy, served with steamed basmati rice.
